on the wartorn planet sera, humanity fights for survival against the
monstrous locust horde, an underground species that emerged without
warning. marcus fenix, a disgraced soldier imprisoned for abandoning
his post, is given a second chance when his best friend, dom santiago,
frees him to rejoin delta squad. tasked with deploying the lightmass
bomb to wipe out the locust tunnels, marcus and his team battle through
devastated cities, underground caverns, and relentless enemy forces. as
they push forward, they uncover the true horrors of the locust and the
desperate lengths humanity must go to in order to survive.
